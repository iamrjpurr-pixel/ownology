# NZ Wine Regulatory Research Notes

## Key Acts & Regulations

### Wine Act 2003 (NZ)
- Primary legislation for winemaking in NZ
- Administered by MPI (Ministry for Primary Industries)
- Requires all winemakers making wine for trade/retail to register a **Wine Standards Management Plan (WSMP)** with MPI
- WSMP must be annually audited ("verified")
- **Exemption:** Winemakers making <20,000 litres over 2 years AND not exporting are exempt (must apply to MPI)
- No longer requires a winemaking "licence" — WSMP replaced it
- URL: https://www.legislation.govt.nz/act/public/2003/114/en/latest/

### Food Act 2014 (NZ)
- Applies to certain winemaking extension activities and partial processing
- Requires food control plans for some activities
- URL: https://www.legislation.govt.nz/act/public/2014/32/latest/

### Sale and Supply of Alcohol Act 2012 (NZ)
- Governs on-site sales, cellar door tastings, and retail of wine
- Requires an **On-Licence** for cellar door tastings and on-site consumption
- Requires an **Off-Licence** for retail sales to take away
- Alcohol Licensing Reform Bill (2026) aims to streamline requirements
- Administered by local councils (District Licensing Committees) and Alcohol Regulatory and Licensing Authority (ARLA)
- URL: https://www.legislation.govt.nz/act/public/2012/120/en/latest/

### Geographical Indications (Wine and Spirits) Registration Act 2006 (NZ)
- Governs use of GIs like Marlborough, Hawke's Bay, Central Otago, etc.
- 18 wine regions registered as GIs
- Administered by IPONZ (Intellectual Property Office of NZ)
- URL: https://www.legislation.govt.nz/act/public/2006/0060/latest/

### Food (Safety) Regulations 2002 (NZ)
- Labelling requirements for wine sold in NZ
- Variety claims: wine must contain ≥75% of that variety
- Vintage claims: wine must contain ≥75% from that vintage year
- GI claims: wine must contain ≥85% from that region
- URL: https://www.legislation.govt.nz/regulation/public/2002/0396/latest/

### FSANZ Food Standards Code (joint AU/NZ)
- Applies in NZ as well as Australia
- Standard 2.7.1 (Spirits), Standard 1.2.1 (Labelling), Standard 1.2.3 (Allergens)
- Pregnancy warning label mandatory from 31 July 2023 (same as AU)
- URL: https://www.foodstandards.gov.au/food-standards-code

### Health and Safety at Work Act 2015 (NZ)
- Primary WHS legislation for NZ
- Administered by WorkSafe NZ
- Requires PCBU (Person Conducting a Business or Undertaking) to eliminate or minimise risks
- Confined spaces (CO2 during fermentation), hazardous substances (SO2) key winery risks
- URL: https://www.legislation.govt.nz/act/public/2015/0070/latest/

### Resource Management Act 1991 (NZ) — RMA
- Governs land use, water use, and environmental discharges
- Winery wastewater discharge to land or water requires a **discharge permit** from regional council
- New national wastewater discharge standards from December 2025
- Planning/land use consent required for new winery buildings or expansions
- Administered by regional councils and district councils
- URL: https://www.legislation.govt.nz/act/public/1991/69/en/latest/

### Hazardous Substances and New Organisms Act 1996 (NZ) — HSNO
- Governs handling of hazardous substances including SO2, pesticides, cleaning chemicals
- Administered by WorkSafe NZ and EPA NZ
- URL: https://www.legislation.govt.nz/act/public/1996/0030/latest/

## Export Requirements (NZ)
- Must comply with Wine Act 2003
- Must register with MPI as an exporter
- Wine must have current export eligibility via MPI's **Wine e-Cert** system (transitioning to MPI Trade Certification)
- All wine in a DTC (direct to consumer) consignment must be registered in Wine e-Cert prior to export
- Destination market requirements vary — check MPI's market access database
- Non-compliance must be reported to MPI within 24 hours
- URL: https://www.mpi.govt.nz/export/food/exporting-new-zealand-grape-wine/steps-to-exporting-new-zealand-grape-wine

## NZ Tax — No WET Equivalent
- NZ does not have a Wine Equalisation Tax (WET)
- Wine is subject to standard GST (15%) and excise duty
- Excise duty administered by NZ Customs Service
- URL: https://www.customs.govt.nz/

## Biosecurity (NZ)
- Biosecurity Act 1993 (NZ) governs pest and disease management
- Phylloxera management plans required in affected regions
- Administered by MPI Biosecurity NZ
- URL: https://www.legislation.govt.nz/act/public/1993/0095/latest/
