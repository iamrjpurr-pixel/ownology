# Boutique Winery — Equipment Cost Breakdown & Australian Supplier Reference
*5–10 tonne vintage scale. All prices in AUD (inc. GST unless noted). Ranges reflect new vs good used condition.*
*Last updated: May 2026. Prices are indicative — always request current quotes.*

---

## How to Read This Document

Each zone from the equipment brainstorm (`boutique-winery-equipment.md`) is reproduced here with three additional columns:

- **Est. Cost (AUD)** — realistic range for new equipment; used/ex-demo can reduce by 30–60%
- **Key Australian Suppliers** — who to call first
- **Notes** — buying tips, lead times, alternatives
