## Equipment Cleaning Protocols

### Big Mouth Bubbler (Primary Fermenter)
- **After each use:** Rinse immediately with warm water. Soak with One Step solution for 5 minutes. Rinse with sulphite solution. Air dry upside down.
- **Before use:** Sanitise with sulphite solution or One Step. No rinse needed for One Step.
- **Inspect:** Check for scratches (harbour bacteria). Replace if scratched.

### Glass Carboy (Secondary Fermenter)
- **After each use:** Rinse with warm water immediately (dried wine is very hard to remove). Use carboy brush with One Step solution. Rinse with sulphite solution. Store dry with bung loosely in neck.
- **Before use:** Sanitise with sulphite solution; drain but do not rinse.
- **Inspect:** Check for chips or cracks around neck. Discard if cracked.

### Auto Siphon & Tubing
- **After each use:** Flush with warm water, then One Step solution. Hang tubing vertically to drain and dry.
- **Before use:** Flush with sulphite solution; drain.
- **Replace:** Tubing every 1–2 years or if discoloured/cracked.

### Hydrometer & Test Jar
- **After each use:** Rinse with warm water. Wipe hydrometer gently with soft cloth. Air dry.
- **Before use:** Rinse with sulphite solution.
- **Inspect:** Check hydrometer for chips (affects accuracy). Replace if chipped.

### Wine Thief
- **After each use:** Disassemble 3-piece unit. Rinse with warm water. Soak in One Step solution. Rinse with sulphite solution. Air dry.

### Airlock & Bung
- **After each use:** Remove airlock water. Rinse with warm water. Soak in One Step solution. Rinse with sulphite solution. Store dry.
- **Before use:** Fill airlock with sulphite solution (not plain water — sulphite prevents contamination if it gets sucked back).
- **Replace:** Bung if cracked or hardened. Airlock if discoloured.

### Wine Bottles
- **Before use:** Rinse with warm water. Soak in sulphite solution using funnel. Drain upside down on bottle tree or rack. Do not rinse after sulphite soak.
- **Corks:** Soak in sulphite solution for 20 minutes before corking. Do not boil (softens cork).

### Degassing Whip
- **After each use:** Rinse with warm water. Soak in One Step solution. Rinse with sulphite solution. Air dry.
- **Inspect:** Check whip blades for cracks or discolouration.
